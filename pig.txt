Pig Latin is not a true language
It is a game in which the beginning consonant or consonant sound of each word is transferred to the end of the word along with the syllable ay
Invented language is a phenomenon that stretches across cultures
Pig Latin seems to have been invented by American children sometime in the nineteenth century
Pig Latin solidified its place in the American consciousness with the release of the song pig Latin Love
The reference to Latin is a deliberate misnomer 
Pig Latin is simply a form of jargon unrelated to Latin
It is most often used by young children as a fun way to confuse people unfamiliar with Pig Latin
